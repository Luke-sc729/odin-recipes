Protein Shake

Ingridients

  • Whey chocolate protein powder
  • Peanut butter
  • Banana
  • Blueberrys
  • Rasberrys
  • Milk

Method

Add two full scoops of protein powder into your blender. Add one mid-sized teaspoons of peanut butter to your blender. Add a handful of blueberrys and rasberrys to your blender. Add your milk and blend thouroghly!
