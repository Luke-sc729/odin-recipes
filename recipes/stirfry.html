Stir Fry

Ingridients

  • Meat of choice (cow or chicken)
  • Stir fry bag
  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce

Method

"Cut meat of choice into thin strips. In a pan, add olive oil and tow cloves of crushed garlic and bring to med heat, add meat to pan. As the pan heats, start preparing the rice in the rice cooker Add a teaspoon off soy sauce to the cooked meat and add your stir-fry bag. (Optional at this point you can add the pan lid if it has one to steam the vegtables aswell) As vegtables soften add more soy sauce to taste, trying to get all ingridients coated. Plate your stir fry on top your rice and garnich with seseme seeds (optional)"
