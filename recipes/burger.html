Burger Recipie

Ingridients

  • 20% Fat beef 250g
  • Burger Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Tomatos
  • Burger Sauce

Seasonings

  • Smoked Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Ground Black Pepper

Method

Mix a tsp of all seasonings into a bowl with half and extra tsp of brown sugar. Roll your mince into 6 even balls being sure to add seasoning as you got. The mince should take on a dark reddish brown colour. Press the mince into pattys and make a small indentation in the middle (this helps while cooking) then put them in the fridge for 20 min. In a pan, add oil and bring to a high heat. Place the burgers and flip only once after they have seered. add salt as you flip them. Then lower the temprature and allow the inside of the burger to cook Toast your bunds and add the aformentiond toppings, burger sauce, oinions, lettuce and sliced Tomatos (Optional you can fry your onions with the burgers for more flavour) Take burger out of pan, plate up with some frys and enjoy!
